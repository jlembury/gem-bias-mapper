Geospatial Ethics in Motion (GEM)

The GEM project investigates how data and algorithmic biases shape our understanding of human mobility, aiming to inform decision-making that better reflects the lived experiences of diverse communities.

This dashboard explores how sampling bias in mobile location data varies across space and sociodemographic dimensions.
